Switching video drivers

Published: Fri 17 February 2017
By Robert Sare

In Updates.

tags: updates system display

There is a display issue in the Benuku VNC related to the NVIDIA driver on hilley-agisoft-linux.

OpenGL will not render Photoscan interface items correctly when the NVIDIA 340.101 driver for our GeForce 740 series graphics card is loaded.

We plan to switch to the Nouveau open source driver in the near future.
